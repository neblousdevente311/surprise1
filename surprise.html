somewhere between the softest
of your breaths and
the moon that leans
to listen (i have
discovered) a million tiny
reasons why
i love you

(i love you more than words have colors
more than mornings have newness
more than sunsets feel like honey
or the oceans have secrets
that whisper only to you)

and when you laugh—oh!
the world tilts a little closer
to perfection

if i were a tree, you’d be the earth
i’d root myself so deeply in
your gentle warmth
your every smile is a sun
that makes me grow taller into
whoever i am meant to be

(i would ask the stars to wait a lifetime
just to see your face one more time)

and though i may not ask
the question straight, love
in all the silences
between our words, know this:
there is a forever
that waits
in every heartbeat
and in every heartbeat
is a whisper
(yours alone)

if the moon ever said “marry me,”
i think i’d say yes

because you are my moon—
the loveliest i have ever known
